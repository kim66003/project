Number of terrorist incidents worldwide

This worldmap shows the number of terrorist attacks for every country for the selected year. The default year is set on 2000. Another year within the period 1990-2017 can be chosen with the slider. The currently selected country, year and donut category are shown in the navbar. The darker the colour of the country, the more terrorist attacks took place that year. The countries have a hover and onclick function to reveal more information about attacks in this country. Countries can also be selected in the dropdown menu. To change the category shown in the donut chart, simply select another in the dropdown menu.
